活動

大會議程
請參考 PyCon TW 2020 議程。
專業課程 (Tutorial)
專業課程是 PyCon Taiwan 的一部分，地點在大會會場旁的好想工作室。只要有購票都有資格參與，無需另外付費，但需要另外報名，以便我們做現場人數控制；專業課程的時間為 90 分鐘。詳情請參考專業課程頁面。

在參加專業課程前，必須先在主會場成功大學國際會議廳完成報到。

Threshold Cryptography with Python
Build a interactive plot website using Plotly/Dash
How to develop ML APIs with Python from Online Learning Dataset
開放空間 (Open Spaces)
在大會的兩天，一樓多功能廳內部會擺設數張桌子，代表不同的開放空間，每張桌子上面都會有個看板，看板即為該空間討論的主題。你可以找到你有興趣的主題，隨時使用一旁的便利貼給予一些想法或是回饋。如果找不到你想討論的主題，那就直接在還沒主題的空間上面填寫吧！詳細資訊請參考這裡
閃電秀 (Lightning Talks)
顧名思義即為「閃電一般短的演講」。包含準備與連接投影機的時間在內，每位講者僅有 5 分鐘的時間。講者必須以飛快的速度，進行簡潔扼要的分享。要是沒能在 5 分鐘結束，會被直接切斷投影機訊號！
如果要報名閃電秀，請在註冊檯的「閃電秀」罐中投入你的講題與姓名，我們會在每天中午抽出當日的中選名單。沒有中選的講題會留在罐子裡，所以越早投稿，機會越大！
社群軌 (Community Track)
社群軌是 PyCon TW 的一項新活動。我們選定數個熱門的主題，並且將同主題的精采演講打包成 5 場連續放送的形式，讓會眾能聽得過癮。我們捨棄了儘管專業但是死板的會議室，在有特色的咖啡廳、展演空間、甚至是古蹟內來舉辦上述各種主題的演講，讓會眾能更加舒適地且近距離地與他人互動。
詳細的資訊在這裡，由於場地大小有限制，請先行在詳細資訊連結上登記想去的社群軌地點以便分流。到時候也會有交通接駁車的服務喔！
周六晚會 (PyNight)
在大會的第一天晚上六點開始，會議廳週邊將充滿歡樂輕鬆的氣氛，有隨手可取的餐點飲食，讓你盡情和同好交流。預計接近 20:50 晚會結束。過往類似的活動記錄可參考這裡，可說是 Python 版本的 BoF Birds of a feather。
大地遊戲：集點抽書
大會期間，您可以至各攤位與廠商互動，完成攤位主的小任務，同時持 App 提供您的 QR code 經過攤位主認證掃描後完成戳章集點。當您集完所有攤位的戳章後，就能參與「 抽獎活動 」來獲得天瓏書局 、知名 Python 教學網站 RealPython 與今年講者兼 Mastering Python Networking 一書作者 Eric Chou 所贊助的書籍，以及大會提供的各大 Python Conference 票券補助。我們將在第一天下午、第二天早上和中午公佈獲獎人名單哦！

快來掃描自己的 QR code 開始參與遊戲吧：https://game.pycon.tw/ ！

就業博覽會 (Job Fair)
這是一個專門為 Python 工程師而設的就業博覽會。Job Fair 雖不會安排任何議程但開放廠商在台上自我介紹，會眾可以趁此時與現場的廠商交流、投履歷，廠商也會藉此時宣傳自己徵才的需求。對於想找工作的會眾，我們鼓勵在這時段多與廠商互相交流，也許美好的機會就此出現。想找工作的你，也不要錯過瀏覽 PyCon TW 官方網站上的徵才牆 唷。

PyCon Taiwan COVID-19 Guidelines

Registration process:

  1. Measure the body temperature at the entrance and alcohol disinfection

Events

Conference Schedule
Please refer to https://tw.pycon.org/2020/conference/schedule/
Tutorials (Workshops)
Tutorials are events held in the Goodideas Studio. No extra charge is required for tutorials, but attendees need to register separately in order to join. They are 1.5 hours events held to help participants better understand talks during the conference, or get their hands on more Python applications. More information about tutorials can be found on the Tutorials page. Note that you'll need to check-in in the International Conference Room before you can join the tutorials.

Threshold Cryptography with Python
Build a interactive plot website using Plotly/Dash
How to develop ML APIs with Python from Online Learning Dataset
Open Spaces
During the conference, there will be several tables inside the Multifunction Room (1F). The tables act as an Open Space. There will be an empty board on each table as its topic. You can find topics interest you, and use the memo to write down your thoughts and feedback. If a topic is not listed yet, just write down what you would like to talk about on a non-topic board! Please refer to https://docs.google.com/document/d/13y1eja-dyBz-zd-nJe1Agh_7RFt10dfPcMAvaqeRnKQ/edit?usp=sharing for detailed information.
Lightning Talks
Lightning talks are held at the end of each conference day. It’s known for its fast-paced nature that allows only 5 minutes for each talk, including setup. If you would like to give a lightning talk, please write down your name and the subject on a piece of paper, and put it into the jar near the registration desk. We will announce at each noon the list of talks for the day. The earlier you register, the higher your chances are.
Community Track
The community track is a new event in PyCon TW. We choose the talks with specific topics and package them into the same session. We'll leave the conference rooms and go to characteristic cafes, exhibition spaces, and even monuments so that the attendees and speakers can interact with each other more comfortably and closely.
More information and beautiful pictures can be found on the Community Track Page. Each venue has its own limit, so please register which track you'd like to go in the Community Track Page so each venue will be balanced.
Certainly, we also provide the bus shuttle service!
PyNight
We will be holding a PyNight on the first day of the conference at 18:00, there will be a social event on the 1st floor. We have delicious buffet on the table and eye-opening performances lining up. The atmosphere is relaxing. Enjoy the fun. Please refer to https://tw.pycon.org/2015apac/en/program/night-party/ for previous records. It is very similar to BoF (birds of a feather) event for Python.
Game Time: Collect the booth's stamp!
During the conference, you can interact with the sponsors at each booth to complete small quests. Meanwhile, you can also provide your QR code to the booth owner with the App. After being scanned by the booth owner, it will be regarded as completing the stamp collection. And after you collect the stamps of all the booths, you can participate in the "raffle" to win the select books sponsored by the "天瓏書局(Tenlong Bookstore)”, "Python Tricks" sponsored by RealPython, and "Mastering Python Networking" (zh version) sponsored by our speaker Eric Chou. We will also offer PyConXX ticket compensation up to 5000 TWD as reward of the raffle. The list of winners will also be announced on the 1st day evening, and in the morning and noon on the second day! So come scan your QR code and start the game: https://game.pycon.tw/ !
Job Fair
Job Fair is a career expo for Python developers. During the Job Fair session, our sponsors will take turns to introduce themselves on the stage and promote their job openings. For attendees that are looking for a career, this is a great opportunity for you to interact with the sponsors or submit your resume! Check our Job listings on PyConTW official website.
