活動

大會議程
請參考 PyConTw2019 議程
閃電秀（Lightning Talks）
閃電秀在會議每天下午結束之前舉辦，是每個演講包含設置投影片僅限五分鐘的刺激活動。如果要報名閃電秀，請在註冊櫃檯的「閃電秀」罐中投入你的講題與姓名，我們會在每天中午抽出當日的中選名單。沒有中選的講題會留在罐子裡，所以越早投稿，機會越大！
周六晚會（PyNight / BoF）
在大會的第二天晚上六點開始，會議廳週邊將充滿歡樂輕鬆的氣氛，有音樂會、鋼琴演奏、隨手可取的餐點飲食，讓你盡情和同好交流。預計接近 20:50 晚會結束。過往類似的活動記錄可參考 這裡 ，可說是 Python 版本的 BoF Birds of a feather。您可使用這個 HackMD 頁面來查詢有多少 BoF 議題可以參與，您也可以自由建立新的、有興趣的議題找人一起交流。HackMD網址
開放空間（Open Spaces）
在大會的第三天，交誼廳兩側走廊會擺設數張桌子，代表不同的開放空間，每張桌子上面都會有個看板，看板即為該空間討論的主題。你可以找到你有興趣的主題，隨時使用一旁的便利貼給予一些想法或是回饋。如果找不到你想討論的主題，那就直接在還沒主題的空間上面填寫吧！詳細資訊請參考 這裡
大地遊戲：集點抽書
在會期間，您可以至各攤位與廠商互動，完成攤位主的小任務，同時持 App 提供您的 QR code 給個攤位主。而在經過攤位主認證掃描後，即視為完成戳章集點，當您集完所有攤位的戳章後，就能參與「 抽獎活動 」來獲得 「 天瓏書局 」所贊助的精選書籍，而我們也將在第二天和第三天早上以及第三天中午公佈獲獎人名單哦！

快來掃描自己的 QR code 開始參與遊戲吧：https://game.pycon.tw/ ！

就業博覽會（Job Fair）
這是一個專門為 Python 工程師而設的就業博覽會。Job Fair 雖不會安排任何議程但開放廠商在台上自我介紹，會眾可以趁此時與現場的廠商交流、投屨歷，廠商也會藉此時宣傳自己徵才的需求。對於想找工作的會眾，我們鼓勵在這時段多與廠商互相交流，也許美好的機會就此出現。

PyCon Taiwan 2019 is held on september 20th and it is approaching! We’re really excited to see you at the conference! We’ve prepared some useful information as the following, please take time and read them carefully:

Transportation

The conference venue is located at Humanities and Social Sciences Building, Academia Sinica (128 Academia Road, Section 2, Nankang, Taipei). Please visit PyCon TW 2019’s official website or campus map of Academia Sinica for detailed information about the location.。

Shuttle Bus

To take the conference shuttle bus, please meet at the bus station in front of Exit 1 of MRT Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center Station between 8:00 and 9:00.

There are also shuttle bus to MRT Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center Station after conferen in each day.

Registration

The starting time of the registration on Set 20 will be 08:30 am. After arriving at the conference venue, please connect to the conference WiFi in order to successfully register through pressing the registration button in “OPass” app. There will be available, first come first serve, and limited amount of breakfast for early bird registration.

Please download OPass through the following personalised link, and open it:


Your personalised link


Please register at the reception and follow the instruction to collect the goodie bag on the 3F.


Notice, your identification badge is provided inside goodie bag, you’ll have to pick-up and wear your badge in order to enter the conference rooms.


If the registration button in OPass is not functioning, or if you failed to successfully install OPass, please show your KKTIX registration QR Code to the staff at the goodie bags booths at either floor, that way you’ll be able to register and pick-up your goodie bag at the same time.

Events

Conference Schedule
Please refer to https://tw.pycon.org/2019/events/schedule/
Lightning Talks
Lightning talks are held at the end of each conference day. It’s known for its fast-paced nature that allows only 5 minutes for each talk, including setup. If you would like to give a lightning talk, please write down your name and the subject on a piece of paper, and put it into the jar near the registration desk. We will announce at each noon the list of talks for the day.
PyNight
On the second night 18:00 of the conference, there will be a social event on the 4th floor. We have delicious buffet on the table and eye-opening performances lining up. The atmosphere is relaxing. Enjoy the fun. Please refer to https://tw.pycon.org/2015apac/en/program/night-party/ for previous records. It is very similar to BoF (birds of a feather) event. You may check this page to see how many BoF topic you are interested in. Besides, it is welcome to create your own BoF topic to have fun with your friends. HackMD Address
Open Spaces
On the third day of the conference, there will be several tables on the aisles beside R4 (4th floor). The tables act as an Open Space. There will be a board on each table as its topic. You can find topics interest you, and use the memo to write down your thoughts and feedback. If a topic is not listed yet, just write down what you would like to talk about on a non-topic board! Please refer to https://tw.pycon.org/2019/en-us/events/open-spaces/ for detailed information.
Game Time: Collect the booth's stamp!
During the conference, you can interact with the sponsors at each booth to complete small quests. Meanwhile, you can also provide your QR code to the booth owner with the App. After being scanned by the booth owner, it will be regarded as completing the stamp collection. And after you collect the stamps of all the booths, you can participate in the "raffle" to win the select books sponsored by the "天瓏書局(Tenlong Bookstore)”. The list of winners will also be announced on the second and third mornings and at noon on the third day! So come scan your QR code and start the game: https://game.pycon.tw/ !
Job Fair
Job Fair is a career expo for Python developers. During the Job Fair session, our sponsors will take turns introduce themselves on the stage and promote their job openings. For attendees that are looking for a career, this is a great opportunity for you to interact with the sponsors or submit your resume!
